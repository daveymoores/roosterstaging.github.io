Easy for parents, fun for kids.

We believe in the power of pocket money to teach the value of money. RoosterMoney lets kids keep track of money and save for the things they love, while parents oversee what money goes in and out of their account.

Parents remain the Bank of Mum and Dad without making deposits, while kids take control of their money.

Every member of the family has an account.



We keep things all fair and square. Kids know how much pocket money they have in their account. Parents get an overview on what pocket money their family holds.

Create Goals to Save For

We all have things we want to save for. Upload goals to save for, that could be a bike, trainers or to add to their Savings Account.

When kids reach their goal, parents can approve and buy the goal.

No more Shopping Showdowns

If kids want to get something while out and about, they can take responsibility for the decision as to whether they buy something or not. Power to kids, no pestering for parents.
