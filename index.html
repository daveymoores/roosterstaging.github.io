Pocket Money Sorted

Transform the way you manage money with your family

Old School Values. New Methods.

Our mission is a simple one. We provide easy and engaging tools that empower parents to raise money-wise kids.

RoosterMoney was born out of Roosterbank, a virtual world we built back in 2013. We’re building a product and platform that will change the way in which kids manage and learn about money. We are also working closely with schools, coding clubs and other financial organisations to create immersive experiences for kids learning about money.

We set out to sort a specific problem: Pocket money is a great idea in principle. But finding a system that works for your family is hard, particularly when you’re juggling the weekly shop, work emails and football practice. And everything else for that matter.

We believe in the power of pocket money to transform children’s understandings of money – whether you give them 20p a week or £10. And we think these lessons are best learned by keeping things simple for parents and fun for kids. Importantly we think the best things are learned together.

We love using the latest technologies to find solutions. We like hacking, making, experimenting and building because we think that’s the best way to learn. If you’re interested in finding out more about us, want to give us some feedback, hook up a partnership, want to work at RoosterMoney or just want to say hi, get in touch!
